Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026 · Last updated: 1 January 2026

1. Who we are

Crew Allowance ("we", "us", "our") is a service that helps IndiGo airline crew members calculate and verify their monthly allowance entitlements. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and application at crewallowance.in.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@crewallowance.in.

2. Information we collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, IndiGo employee ID, and rank.

Flight data: When you use the calculator, you upload CSV files containing your logbook and schedule data. This data is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Payment information: Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card number, expiry date, or CVC. We receive only a transaction confirmation from Stripe.

Usage data: We may collect basic analytics such as pages visited and features used, to improve the service. This does not include your flight data.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send you account-related emails (password resets, billing confirmations)
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and maintain the service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your flight data for any purpose other than performing calculations within your browser session.

4. Your flight data

The CSV files you upload for allowance calculation are processed entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. These files are never uploaded to our servers and are not retained after your session ends.

We have no access to your logbook entries, flight times, routes, or any data contained in your AIMS exports.

5. Data storage and security

Your account data (name, email, employee ID, rank) is stored securely using Supabase, which is hosted on AWS infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS, database row-level security policies, and secure authentication via Supabase Auth. We will notify you promptly in the event of a data breach that affects your personal information.

6. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and database (supabase.com)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com)
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment (vercel.com)
  • Anthropic — AI processing for admin rate extraction (anthropic.com)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns about how they handle data.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@crewallowance.in and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use session cookies solely to keep you signed in between visits. We do not use tracking cookies or serve advertising. No third-party advertising networks have access to your data through our site.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
