The five PAH allowances

PAH §1.0
Deadhead Allowance
₹4,000 per scheduled block hour for Captains, ₹2,000 for First Officers, DHF sectors only.
Ready
PAH §2.0
TLPD / Domestic Layover
₹3,000 flat for 10:01–24:00 hours away from home base, +₹150/hour beyond.
Ready
PAH §6.0
Tail Swap Allowance
₹1,500 per qualifying same-duty aircraft change. DHT excluded, DHF→DHF excluded.
Coming soon
PAH §7.0
Transit Halt Allowance
₹1,000/hr capped at 4 hours, on the 90-minute rule with a 15-minute difference exception.
Coming soon
PAH §9.0
Night Flying Allowance
₹2,000/night hour, calculated from scheduled departure (STD), 00:01–06:00 IST window.
